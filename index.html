Automata Theory App

Automata Theory App provides access to a brief summary of subjects from automata theory and formal languages. Main definitions, facts, examples and exercises with solutions are provided. Automata Theory App can be used as a study guide and resource for quick consultation on your device.

Free solutions with explanations to about 95 exercises are available on the app.
